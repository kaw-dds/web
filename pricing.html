Frequently asked questions

How does the free plan work?

Our free plan includes up to 50 students and 5 active projects. Perfect for trying out EduProject with a single course or small research group.

Can we upgrade during the semester?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate any charges based on your billing cycle.

Do you offer multi-year contracts?

Yes, we offer multi-year contracts with additional discounts. Contact our sales team for custom pricing.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees or hidden costs. We also provide free onboarding and training for all university plans.
